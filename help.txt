

				HOW TO PLAY

		Player 1				Player 2


 Move: 	  	   W					   ^
       	       A   S   D			       <   v   >

 Shoot:		   G					 ENTER

	- In the board of each Player, there are 2 ships and every ship
	is made by 3 cells continuously (vertical or horizontal). 
	- Each player use the navigation keys above to move the cursor
	on the other's board and chose the cell to shoot.
	- Using the [G] and [ENTER] to shoot.
	- The Player who destroy both ships of the other will become the
	winner.
